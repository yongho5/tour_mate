Seoul Tourist Guide

Attractions Restaurants Culture

Main Attractions

Namsan Seoul Tower

Namsan Seoul Tower is a landmark of Seoul where you can enjoy a panoramic view of the city.

Myeongdong

Myeongdong is a lively area famous for shopping, dining, and street performances.

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace is a major royal palace of the Joseon Dynasty, known for its beautiful architecture and rich historical background.

Recommended Restaurants

Gwangjang Market

Gwangjang Market is a famous market offering a variety of traditional foods.

Itaewon

Itaewon is a multicultural area where you can taste a variety of international cuisines.

Cultural Experiences

National Museum of Korea

The National Museum of Korea is a leading museum showcasing Korean history and culture.

Bukchon Hanok Village

Bukchon Hanok Village is an area where traditional hanok houses are well-preserved, allowing visitors to experience Korean traditional culture.
