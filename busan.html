Welcome to Busan

Busan is a bustling city in South Korea, famous for its stunning beaches, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant nightlife. Here are some fun facts about Busan:

  • Busan is South Korea's second-largest city and a major port.
  • It hosts the world's largest department store, Shinsegae Centum City.
  • Haeundae Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Korea.
  • The city is known for its delicious seafood, especially its sashimi.
